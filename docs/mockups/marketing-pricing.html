Frequently Asked Questions

Can I switch plans later?

Yes, upgrade or downgrade anytime. Changes take effect on your next billing cycle. Upgrading gives you instant access to new features.

What happens when I exceed the employee limit?

On the Starter plan, employees over 50 are billed at ₱15/employee/month as overage. We'll notify you before charging. Consider upgrading to Growth for unlimited employees.

Is there a setup fee?

No setup fees, ever. Create your account in minutes and start adding employees immediately. No credit card required for the free plan.

Do you support multiple companies?

Each company gets its own subdomain (e.g., company.payro.ph) and a separate subscription. Contact us at sales@payro.ph for multi-company discounts.
